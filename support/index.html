2. Frequently Asked Questions

What is Led Banner?

Led Banner is a free mobile application that allows you to create and display LED-style banner effects. You can customize text, colors, animations, and effects to create eye-catching banners.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Led Banner is completely free to download and use. The app is supported by advertisements, which help us continue to provide and improve the service.

Does the app collect personal information?

Led Banner does not require user registration or personal information submission. We may collect limited technical data to improve app performance and stability. For detailed information, please see our Privacy Policy.

How do I save or export my banners?

You can save your banner creations directly to your device's storage. The app will request storage permission to save your work. You can also share your banners directly from the app.

Can I use the app offline?

Most features work offline, but some features like advertisements and analytics require an internet connection.

4. Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't open

  • Try restarting your device
  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Check if your device meets the minimum system requirements
  • Clear the app cache (Settings → Apps → Led Banner → Clear Cache)

Camera or microphone not working

  • Check that the app has permission to access camera/microphone in your device settings
  • Make sure no other apps are using the camera or microphone
  • Restart the app after granting permissions

Can't save banners

  • Ensure the app has storage permission enabled
  • Check that you have enough storage space on your device
  • Try saving to a different location if available

Ads are not displaying

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure ad blockers are disabled (if applicable)
  • Wait a few moments and try again

5. Permissions & Settings

Led Banner may request the following permissions to provide full functionality:

  • Camera: To capture and display banner effects with real-time preview
  • Microphone/Audio: To record sound for video features (if applicable)
  • Storage: To save, load, and export your banner creations
  • Internet: To display advertisements and sync analytics (for app improvement)

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time in your device's system settings. Note that some features may not work properly if permissions are denied.
