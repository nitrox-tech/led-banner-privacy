1. Introduction

Led Banner is provided by NitroX as a free mobile application. The Service is offered "as is" without requiring user registration or personal information submission. However, certain non-personal data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated within the app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information (PII). However, when you use the App, limited technical data may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Language and region settings
  • App usage data, crash logs, and performance metrics
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location data (non-precise)

This data is collected to help improve performance, stability, and advertising relevance through third-party tools.

3. How We Use Information

Data collected (either by NitroX or through third-party partners) may be used to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage trends and app performance
  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Maintain and secure the Service

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with any unauthorized third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The Led Banner app integrates with trusted third-party tools for analytics, advertising, and app performance. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Ads (Marketing & Analytics)
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crash Reporting)
  • Facebook (Advertising & Social Features)
  • Unity (Ads & Game Analytics)

5. Device Permissions

The App may request access to certain features or data on your device. You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time in your device's system settings:

  • Camera: Capture and display banner effects
  • Microphone / Audio: Record sound for certain video features
  • Storage: Save, load, or export banners and media files
  • Internet: Display ads and connect to analytics services

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

While NitroX does not use cookies directly, some third-party SDKs integrated into the App may use cookies or tracking identifiers to collect analytical data or deliver personalized ads. You may disable ad personalization or cookies via your device or Google/Apple account settings.

7. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no electronic transmission or storage system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided information, we will delete it immediately.
